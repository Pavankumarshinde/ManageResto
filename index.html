How To Use ManageResto

1. Categories & Menu
Go to the Menu tab to add your food categories (e.g., Starters, Main Course) and then add specific dishes under them.

2. Taking Orders
Go to the Orders tab, click the big + Create Order button. Select your items, adjust quantities, and place the order.

3. Waiters & Tables
When creating an order, you can optionally assign it to a table number and a specific waiter. Manage waiters in the Orders screen footer.

4. Billing
On any active order, select "Bill / Print". You can add tax/discounts/tips before printing the final receipt.

5. Analytics
The Analytics tab shows your daily/monthly sales, top items, and waiter performance metrics.
